How to Use Transaction Categorizer
  1. Click on the "Choose File" button to upload your transaction file in .xlsx or .xls format. You can upload multiple
  2. Review the transactions displayed in the table.
  3. Use the dropdown in the "Category" column to assign categories to transactions. It will also auto assign other transactions with the same description.
  4. View the "Category Summary" and "Monthly Breakdown" for insights.
  5. Click "Clear Categories" to reset all assigned categories.
